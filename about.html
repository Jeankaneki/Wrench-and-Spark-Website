Our Story

Wrench & Spark was founded in 2023 by a group of veteran mechanics who were tired of seeing fellow professionals struggle with subpar tools. We believe that every job, big or small, deserves the reliability and precision that only comes from high-quality equipment.

Starting from a small garage in Cape Town, we dedicated ourselves to sourcing, testing, and supplying only the tools we would trust in our own bays. Today, Wrench & Spark has grown into a leading supplier for workshops and DIY enthusiasts across the country, but our core mission remains the same: to equip you with tools that work as hard as you do.
